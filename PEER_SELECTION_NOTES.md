- **Jenoptik (JEN.DE)**: Photonics and optical systems overlap.
- **Huber+Suhner (HUBN.SW)**: Connectivity and industrial cabling exposure.
- **NKT (NKT.CO)**: Power/subsea cable and electrification exposure.
- **Mersen (MRN.PA)**: Electrical components and power management peer.
